    Skills¶

    The following skills are available in the base system of Q*:

    Might Skills¶

    Athletics¶

    is used to perform actions such as jumping, climbing, tumbling, lifting, dragging or rope walking. Athletics is also used to move out of harms way. It can be used to dodge hazards such as a falling debris.

     

    • You fall prone
    • You slip and fall
    • Your throw hits an obstacle and bounces back

     

    • placeholder

    Breaching¶

    is used to break open containers, doors, etc. Some things cannot be breached without proper tools, explosives or other aids.

     

    • The tool used breaks
    • The door gets stuck or only bends
    • Debris hurts you

     

    • placeholder

    Melee¶

    is the skill involved with all sorts of melee weapons, such as blades, axes, clubs or bare fists and kicks. It’s also used for defending against a melee attack, either by deflecting, blocking or dodging an attack.

     

    • You stumble and fall
    • You lose the grip of your weapon

     

    • You disarm your opponent

    Micro Gravity¶

    is the skill that replaces Athletics in micro gravity environments. It is used to manoeuvre in such weightless conditions.

     

    • You’re too fast and take damage
    • You’re too slow and need longer to reach

    Heavy Weapons¶

    allows operations of heavy or stationary guns. Such guns are usually mounted turrets on ships and tanks, but can also be land based artillery or very large man portable weapons such as rocket launchers or guided missile platforms. Extra ability modifiers can be spend on either extra normal (1 modifier per point) or critical damage (1 modifier per two points).

    Light Weapons¶

    is used to operate firearms such as pistols, rifles, or shotguns. Light weapons also applies to man portable weapons such as grenade launchers or light machine guns. Extra ability modifiers can be spent on extra damage. Extra ability modifiers can be spend on either extra normal (1 modifier per point) or critical damage (1 modifier per two points).

    Lockpicking¶

    is used for opening closed locks. Some doors cannot be lockpicked, some may require additional tools such as special lockpicking tools, others can be used by opened by hand. The skill assumes, that the character has simple, improvised tools to pick locks. Lockpicking is also used to break into objects such as safes.

    Intelligence Skills¶

    Engineering¶

    is used to construct and repair devices, buildings and vehicles. Servicing a motorcycle is considered engineering, as is constructing fortifications or repairing an airlock. Extra ability modifiers can be used to use less materials or complete the action faster.

     

    • placeholder

     

    • placeholder

    Explosives¶

    can be used to disarm, handle or even build explosive ordinance. When explosives are thrown (i.e. grenades) roll Athletics instead. Otherwise defusing and planting explosives is a competition between the one setting it, and the one defusing it.

     

    • The trigger for the explosive device fails
    • The explosion is delayed by some time

     

    • You need less materials for crafting explosives

    First Aid¶

    is used to treat wounds and cure health damage with the help of a medical item. A first aid check requires a first aid kit (or similar) and doubles the amount of dice rolled for health and endurance if the check is successful. If the check fails the normal amount of dice are applied as healing. The game master may also limit how often first aid can actually benefit a character depending on the severity of the wounds. See medical items for examples.

     

    • You lose the medical item without any health restored

     

    • Add 1d6 to the amount of health restored

    Hacking¶

    is used to bypass electronic security. Its counter skill is also hacking. Extra ability modifiers can be used to get the job done faster.

     

    • You trigger a system lockdown

     

    • You gain full access to the system

    Knowledge¶

    is chosen for a particular area, e.g. history, religion, etc. For all other knowledge skills, the character only has basic, common knowledge.

    Science¶

    is chosen for a particular scientific field, e.g. geology, biology, astronomy, medicine, etc. For all other scientific areas that weren’t chosen, the character has basic knowledge. They only know common knowledge in these areas (according to their education), but nothing special.

    Charisma Skills¶

    Back story¶

    allows you to determine if you know other characters or things about them and their life. Knowledge about a character or past acquaintances may improve or worsen relations with that character.

     

    • You have bad reputation amongst the other party

     

    • You’re a friend of the other party

    Oratory¶

    is used to sway another in ones favour through social interactions. It allows a character to bargain, bluff, intimidate or charm another. The counter skill is called Sense Motive.

    allows to spot bluffs in conversations, resist an intimidation attempt, notice lies etc. It is the counter part skill to Oratory.

    Small Unit Tactics¶

    allows to point and interpret wordless commands in combat. The commander has to succeed on a DC based on the command. The receiver also has to succeed the task based on the DC. If the commanders skill check exceeds the DC a lot, the receiver may receive a bonus.
