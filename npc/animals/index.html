    Wolf¶

    A wolf is a ferocious beast, that usually hunts in a pack.

    Organization
    Solitary, pair, or pack (7-16)
    Difficulty
    1
    Attributes
    Body d8, Mind d4, Soul d4
    Skills
    Melee d6, Stealth d8, Search d6, Survival d6
    Combat
    Pace: 10, Attack: d6, Damage: d8, Health: 15
